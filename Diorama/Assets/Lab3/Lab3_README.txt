Description: 
My scene has a large ground plane with cubes which are grabable. The user can move around and perform a scaled world grab with left controller and contineous scale with the
right controller. 

Elements of Scene:
 - Ground plane for objects and player
 - Cubes that are grabbable and can be transformed
 - VR Camera Rig which acts as the camera for the VR headset to control camera gaze to aim with the headset
 - VR Controllers for users to use to control and pick up cubes to perform a scaled world grab 

Controls/Scripts: 

Left Trigger = Hold to perform grab and scale
Right Buttons = Contineous scale and de-scale
